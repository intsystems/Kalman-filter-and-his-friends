Variational Bayesian Kalman Filter
===================================

VB-AKF adaptively estimates the measurement noise covariance :math:`R` online
using a variational Bayesian approach with an inverse-Wishart prior.
Based on `Sarkka & Nummenmaa (2009) <https://users.aalto.fi/~ssarkka/pub/mvb-akf-mlsp.pdf>`__.
